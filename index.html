Paragraph 1 — Classical Guitar

I have been a classical guitarist for a decade and have earned five First Class Honors and two First Class Honors with Distinction awards for my practical exams, and two First Class Honors and two First Class Honors with Distinction awards for my theory exams. Therefore, I have won three regional gold medals from the Royal Conservatory of Music, have performed at University of Southern California's Newman Hall for the two regional golds I won in one academic school year, and will perform at the Colburn School in Los Angeles in March of 2026 due to only placing second in my level in the United States. Additionally, I have performed in multiple other venues, including All Souls' Episcopal Church, California Center for the Arts, and the Jacobs Music Center. In February of 2025, I was selected out of several hundreds of students as the Villa Musica Student of the Month and have served as an inspirational figure for several musicians. I currently play Vicente Carillo's 2024 India Estudio CD/IN using high tension Augustine Regal Crystal Nylon Classical Guitar Strings, as I find the higher tension and durability is able to withstand the power I exert while maintaining a rich sound quality and projecting the sound properly.
